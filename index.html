About us


Welcome to Frankhomes, your number one source for all kinds of houses. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of houses, with a focus on luxury, comfort and security. Founded in 2018 by Frank Khofkosky, FrankHomes has come a long way from its beginnings in Lagos. When Frank first started out, his passion for FrankHomes drove them to do tons of research so that FrankHomes can offer you "the world's most advanced, affordable and secured housing and real-estate services". We now serve customers all over the country, and are thrilled that we're able to turn our passion into our own website.
We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
